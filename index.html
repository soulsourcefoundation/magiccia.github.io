*

Soul Source is active in promoting the empowerment and education of women and youth in Western Kenya. Our goal is active engagement through community empowerment programs, educational support for secondary school, college and university and nursery school. Learn more about Soul Source Foundation.

Empowerment Programs

Holistic Empowerment

We provide funding and training to facilitate Women's empowerment and community support groups around Lake Victoria and in Nairobi.

Financial Empowerment

We provide support and training for economical development through community based microfinance.

Youth Empowerment

Certified empowerment trainers collaborate with school leaders to facilitate empowerment training and mentorship for students and parents.

Education Programs

Nutrition

We provide daily meals for nursery school students and secondary school students in Kamasengre.

Secondary Scholarships

We provide financial support and empowerment training for youth to complete secondary school on Rusinga Island.

Postsecondary Scholarships

We provide funding and mentorship for young women from Rusinga Island to complete college and university.
